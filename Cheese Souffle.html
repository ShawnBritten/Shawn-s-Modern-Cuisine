Cheese Souffle

Item Quantity Preparation Price
Parmesan Cheese 4 tsp finely grated 4.39
Parmesan Cheese 1 cup divided
Milk 1 cup cold 1.87
Nutmeg pinch 1.18
Pepper 1 tsp 1.00
Salt 1 tsp 0.79
Large Egg 4 yolks and whites seperate 1.00
Xanthan Gum (optional) 1/4 tsp 10.49
Total: $23.72

Special Equipment

Four 8-ounce ramekins

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. Brush ramekins with butter and place on a foil-lined baking sheet.
  3. sprinkle ramekins with 2 tablespoons Parmesan (total).
  4. Chill for 20 minutes or, covered, up to 1 day.
  5. Melt 3 tablespoons butter in a small saucepan over medium heat. Add flour; whisk constantly for 30 seconds.
  6. Gradually whisk in milk; increase heat and bring to a boil, whisking constantly.
  7. Add remaining 2 tablespoons Parmesan and 1/2 cup Cheese; stir until melted.
  8. Add nutmeg and season with salt and pepper.
  9. Transfer béchamel to a medium bowl and cover with plastic wrap; set aside.
  10. Place egg whites in a large bowl.
  11. Stir in a pinch of salt and xanthan gum, if using.
  12. Stir egg yolks into béchamel. Gently stir in 1/4 of beaten egg whites to loosen béchamel base, then gently fold in remaining egg whites just to combine, taking care not to deflate.
  13. Divide mixture among ramekins; sprinkle with 1/2 cup Cheese.
  14. Run your finger around inside lip of ramekins, cleaning edges.
  15. Bake until soufflés rise, centers are set, and cheese is golden brown, 18-22 minutes. Serve immediately.
